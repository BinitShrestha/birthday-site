You're Invited!

The DTWells’ 50th Birthday Committee is filled with joy and much excitement as we approach the celebration of a wonderful man, father, brother, son, uncle, teacher, servant and friend!

You have received an invitation by selection, proving to be a person near and dear to the heart of DTW. The festivities we have planned will encompass his most innate passions, Love, Laughter and Living, to the fullest capacity possible. The series of events were created with convenience to ensure your participation on some level, so jump in wherever you can!

Please review the following pages for extensive details surrounding the celebration’s events. If at any point you have a question or need further clarification, please feel free to email the committee members at dtwells50th@gmail.com. For URGENT matters, please contact Carla Briggs at 617-780-2866.

Thank you for your excitement. We cannot wait to see you all this summer!


-The Committee
